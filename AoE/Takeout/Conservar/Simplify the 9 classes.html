Simplify the 9 classes
As a starting place, let's simplify the 9 original classes for testing the prototypes.


Warrior: remove many attacks and utility. Keep the three stances. Keep much more rage when stance dancing. Passives: default expertise is high, gains extra rage when autoattacking. Cc duration is reduced. Dodge chance +5%. Extra threat when using a shield.

Arms: charge (stuns 2sec), hamstring, instant heroic strike (talent, becomes Mortal Strike, so extra damage and heal reduction)

Fury: cleave as an instant attack, execute, Battle and Demo shout mixed into one short AoE buff/debuff, pummel, whirlwind.

Protection: (talent) Cleave becomes Thunderclap, (talent) pummel becomes shield bash, (talent) heroic strike reduces enemy armor %. Taunt (disarms with talent once every 40 seconds), shield slam.


Mage: Shatter, Ignite and Clearcasting are passives. When under 25% mana, consumes a mana gem (only once every 3 min). Gives party mana regen.

Frost: Frost Nova, Frost bolt, Blizzard (slows), Ice Block.

Fire: Pyroblast (3.5 sec), Scorch, Blizzard can become Flamestrike (slows).

Arcane: PoM (1min CD), polymorph, Arcane Intellect, Arcane Explosion, Blink, Counterspell, stronger Mana Shield on a CD, Portal to Hearthstone inn.


Warlock: Demon armor is passive and gives stamina, imps are summoned by procs. Gives party increased health.

Affliction: Curse of Agony (becomes Corruption with 40% heal with talents), Curse of elements, drain mana, Drain life, Life Tap, Fear (can be instant with a proc with talents) (can become Howl of Terror with talents).

Demonology: Summon Voidwalker, Succubus, Felhunter, healthstone ritual, Empowered Health Funnel.

Destruction: Immolate, Shadowbolt (becomes Incinerate with talent), Hellfire (can become demon aura version with talents or a static cast Rainfire), conflagrate.


Rogue: Finishers give attack speed to party. Safe fall. Detect and disarm traps.

Assassination: Dismantle, Cheap Shot, Garrote, Kidney Shot, Rupture, Eviscerate.

Combat: Backstab (can be used from the front for half damage, stealth multiplies its damage and gives an extra combo point, extra dmg with daggers) (can become Swift Strike with talents, which does damage to two other targets but loses the back dmg bonus), Evasion, Gouge (interrupts too, only incapacitates from the front), Sprint

Subtlety: Stealth (increases damage of next Rogue attack), Vanish, Sap


Priest: Gains a periodic shield that grants armor and spell power. Spirit of redemption passive (10 seconds)

Discipline: Prayer of Fortitude, Mana Burn, Shield, Dispel Magic (plus disease).

Holy: Smite, Holy Fire (replaces Smite), Heal, Flash Heal, Holy Nova (heals charge its healing, smite and holy fire charge its damage), Renew

Shadow: Pain, Mind Blast (Replaces Smite), Devouring Plague, Mind Control, Mind Flay (can become Mind Sear by talents), Psychic Scream. Casting 3 shadow spells in a row causes you to enter Shadow Form for 10 seconds (refreshed every time you cast another one and lost upon using holy magic).


Paladin: 10% Increased crit chance against undead, passive aura that gives magic resistance and armor. Increases threat when using a shield.

Holy: Holy Light, Flash of Light, Cleanse, Holy Shock (Replaces Crusader Strike), Consecration, Seal of Righteousness, Lay on Hands

Protection: Blessing of Kings (can be upgraded through talents to give either MP5 or AP), Holy Shield, Hammer of Justice, Divine Shield (can be cast on allies), Hammer of Righteousness (replaces Crusader Strike), Hand of reckoning, Hand of freedom, Seal of Justice (replaces SoR)

Retribution: Judgement, Crusader Strike, SoCommand (replaces SoR), (talent: anyone affected by your auras deals damage as thorns damage (retri aura)), hammer of wrath.



Hunter: Party members have 15% increased speed, chance to shoot an extra arrow or hit with your melee weapon again on hit.

Beast Mastery: Hawk, Monkey, Pet Spells

Marksmanship: Arcane Shot (can become aimed shot +dmg and healing reduction, or otherwise it can become multi-shot), Concussive Shot, Hunter's Mark, Serpent Sting, Steady Shot

Survival: Disengage, Immolation Trap (can become Explosive trap), Feign Death, Freezing Trap (can trigger AoE area that slows), Raptor Strike (instant, can be improved), Wing Clip, Tracking, Scatter Shot (can become wyvern sting or Intimidation).


Druid:

Balance: Entangling Roots, Faerie Fire (castable in Feral Form), Innervate, Moonfire (applies insect swarm with talents), Moonkin Form, Wrath, Starfire.

Feral: Travel Form, Aquatic, Bear, Cat
Bear: Bash, instant Maul (2.5sec CD), Swipe, Charge, demo roar, Growl.
Cat: whatever Cat has

Restoration: Mark of the Wild (applies Thorns too), Cure Poison (and Curse), Healing Touch, Regrowth, Rejuvenation, Tranquility.


Shaman:

Elemental: Chain Lightning, Lightning Bolt, Shock (can be upgrade with talents, baseline is just damage), Earthbind Totem, Magma Totem, Searing Totem, Purge.

Enhancement: Flametongue/Frostband/Windfury Weapon, Ghost Wolf, Lightning Shield, Grounding Totem, Stormstrike, Strength of Earth Totem, Windfury Totem

Restoration: Chain Heal, Healing Wave, Cleansing Totem, Earth Shield, Water Shield, Mana Spring Totem, Mana Tide Totem, Tremor Totem, Riptide.
