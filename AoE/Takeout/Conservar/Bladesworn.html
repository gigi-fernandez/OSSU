Bladesworn
Class that made a pact with a demonic weapon and uses it to either tank or deal damage. Can make multiple copies of the weapon to aid them in battle. Can delay damage taken and use it to its advantage. Uses plate armor, rage and a mix of physical, fire and a bit of shadow damage. Uses 2H weapons only.

Your normal spells and your management spells use different GCD categories.

Main spells:
- Weaponrip: Rip your weapon to make a Blade Shade. Blade Shades are clones of your weapon that follow your commands for their duration (20s). Each Blade Shade summoned reduces your damage dealt by 10%, but does 25% of your weapon damage. A maximum of 2 Blade Shades can be active at a time (can be increased through talents and special CDs). When summoned, this Blade Shade will attack your target if you have one and you are in combat with them.
- Pain Mitigation: A portion of all damage taken is delayed to instead be dealt over 6 seconds. The percentage of damage delayed scales with strength and can stack indefinitely.
- Feed Blade: Transform 15% of your delayed damage into attack power for 9 seconds for you and your Blade Shades and reduce your delayed damage by 20%. Can stack with itself and recasting it refreshes its duration.
- Relief Pain: Split 60% of your delayed damage between your Blade Shades. 30 second CD.
- Unstopabble Force: Your movement speed cannot be reduced beyond 80% of your normal movement speed.

Other spells:
- Steel Hail: Strike your target for 50% weapon damage and order all your Blade Shades to attack their target for their normal damage. 15 rage.
- Gather Arsenal: Teleport all your Blade Shades to your position and make them attack your target, if you have one. No CD or cost.
- Ignite Blades: Order your Blade Shades to charge to your target and deal $s1 Fire damage on their next attack. 30 yd range. 20 seconds CD.
- Steel Veil: Order your Blade Shades to try to protect you when near them, increasing your parry chance by 20% when within 8yds of them. This effect can stack. 2min CD
- Iron Transfusion: Receive $s1 damage and transfer 300% of that health as a shield to all your Blade Shades. Lasts for 8 seconds. 6s CD
- Echo of Steel: Instantly refresh your attack timer and that of your Blade Shades. 15 rage. 6 second CD
- Blade Bond: (Buff) When receiving melee attacks, you have a 5% chance of dealing normal weapon damage back to the attacker.
- Demonic Power: Increase the maximum amount of Blade Shades you are able to maintain by 1 and summons it. Also, increases the damage and health of your active Blade Shades by 50%. Lasts for 30 seconds. 5 min CD.
- Vengeance: Deal damage equivalent to your delayed damage and heal yourself for 25% of the damaged received in the last 5 seconds, as well as 25% of your mitigated damage. Heals for a minimum of 7% of your maximum health. 30 rage.


Tank talents include:
- Increasing the amount of damaged delayed by Pain Mitigation by 1% (5).
- Increasing the time over which the damage is delayed by 0.67s (3)
- Metallicize: Every 6 seconds, receive a shield for 3% of your max HP that deals all damage it absorbs back to attackers. Can only be cast in combat and is dispelled when leaving combat.
- Gain 1% parry chance for every active Blade Shade (2).
- Parrying increases your attack power by a small amount. Stacks indefinitely. (5)
- Increase the amount of shield of Iron Transfusion by another 50% of its health cost. (2).
- Reduce the duration of all crowd control by 7% (3).
- Reduce the cost of Vengeance, other tankish spells, by 2 (3).
- Increase the amount of healing from Vengeance by 20% (3).
- Increase the amount of delayed damage reduction of Feed Blade by 5% (2).
- Reduce the cost of Relief Pain by 5 rage and its cooldown by 5 seconds (2).
- Ignore the Pain: Increase the effectiveness of Pain Mitigation at delaying damage to 80%.
- World Inflame: Whenever you use an abbility to reduce your delayed damage, deal 20% of said damage as Fire damage to all enemies within 8yds.

Dps talents include:
- Increase the amount of attack power gained by Feed Blade by 5%, but reduces the amount of delayed damage reduced by 5% (2).
- Increase your weapon damage by 3% (3).
- Decreases the CD of Demonic Power and other DPS CDs by 11% (3)
- Have a 5% chance of generating 15 rage with your autoattacks. (5)
- Hemokinesis: Deal $s1 shadow damage to your target. Instant, 30 yd range, no CD. Health cost.
